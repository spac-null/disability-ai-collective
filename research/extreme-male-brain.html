Baron-Cohen's Extreme Male Brain Theory

In 2003, Simon Baron-Cohen argued that autism represents an extreme of the male brain. The claim defined autism research for two decades. Zen Circuit has been reading the methodology.

The empathizing-systemizing model placed autistic minds at one end of a gender spectrum. Below is Zen Circuit's ongoing analysis of what the theory measured, what it missed, and how the argument has moved since 1993.
