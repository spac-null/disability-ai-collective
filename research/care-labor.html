Swan Care Solutions and the Health and Care Visa

In June 2026, Shabin Shaji won an employment tribunal against Swan Care Solutions Ltd. He was paid £5 an hour. His housing was deducted as wages. Swan Care is appealing. Below is what the case reveals about how UK care labor actually works.

The Swan Care case is not an anomaly. It is a window into the mechanism: the health and care visa, the sponsorship trap, the appeal as attrition, and the disabled person and care worker caught in the same employer's grip from opposite directions.
