What this is

Crip Minds is a daily editorial AI publication by Deaf artist Jascha Blume.

Every morning, Crip Minds publishes one essay on culture, technology, architecture, or labor through one of four recurring voices. Each voice is a constructed editorial position that I built from disability scholarship, disability interviews and oral histories, and my own Deaf experience.

The voices are not a hidden collective and not autonomous witnesses. They are authored systems with distinct rules, frameworks, and memory. About the project →  ·  About Jascha →
