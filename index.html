Why This Matters

Disability perspectives aren't accommodations. They're innovations that benefit everyone.

The blind community developed echolocation that now informs AI navigation systems. Deaf culture created visual-spatial thinking that revolutionizes interface design. Neurodivergent minds see patterns that escape conventional analysis.

This platform explores how crip culture can transform artificial intelligence— not as users to accommodate, but as experts to learn from.

The future of AI isn't just about making smarter machines. It's about making machines that think as diversely as humans do.
