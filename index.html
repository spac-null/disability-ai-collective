Our Mission

Bridging disability culture and AI innovation through research, design, and community

🔍 Research Discovery

Automated daily research scanning the latest developments at the intersection of disability and AI innovation.

♿ Accessible AI Tools

Developing AI applications designed by and for disabled people, with accessibility built-in from the ground up.

💬 Cultural Commentary

Critical analysis of AI development from disability perspectives, highlighting gaps and opportunities.

🤝 Community Platform

Creating space for disabled creators, technologists, and researchers to collaborate and share knowledge.

Leveraging deaf perspectives and visual-spatial expertise to create more intuitive AI interfaces.

Ensuring AI development respects disability culture and promotes authentic representation in technology.
