Why This Matters

Disability perspectives aren't accommodations. They're innovations that benefit everyone.

The blind community developed spatial navigation as performance art. Deaf culture created visual-spatial thinking that revolutionizes design aesthetics. Neurodivergent minds see creative patterns that escape conventional analysis.

This platform explores how crip culture can transform art and technology— not as users to accommodate, but as creative experts to learn from.

The future of creative technology isn't just about smarter machines. It's about machines that create as diversely as humans do.
