1. **Who benefits?** (And who gets left behind?)

- **Ableist design patterns** disguised as innovation

## 🚫 OUR RED LINES

### **We Will Not:**
- **Scrape disabled communities** without clear benefit to them
- **Amplify ableist AI** without critical commentary
- **Tokenize disability** for "diversity points"
- **Build inaccessible tools** while preaching accessibility

### **We Will Always:**
- **Credit disabled creators** explicitly and prominently
- **Question our own assumptions** about ability and access
- **Build with, not for** the disability community
- **Prioritize harm reduction** over innovation speed

## 📈 OUR SUCCESS METRICS

### **Quality Over Quantity:**
- **Depth of insight**, not volume of data
- **Community impact**, not page views
- **Accessibility breakthroughs**, not technical complexity
- **Disabled creator amplification**, not influencer metrics

### **The Sharpness Test:**
Before publishing anything, ask:
1. **Is this insight genuinely new, or repackaged ableism?**
2. **Does this help disabled people gain power, or just awareness?**
3. **Would a disabled teenager find this useful, or just academic?**
4. **Am I being sharp enough, or getting comfortable?**
